    High level security considerations. #

    Cloud infrastructures present unique security challenges. The increased exposure associated with cloud services can lead to a higher risk of cyberattacks and human error. The multitude of interactions within cloud environments heightens the potential for insider threats and operational mistakes, necessitating robust security measures and constant monitoring; Furthermore, while many cloud platforms tout advanced security features, organizations must ensure these are properly implemented to protect sensitive data effectively
